Welcome to our website. Explore our mission and vision for the future.

Mission

The mission of the Ethiopian Ministry of Innovation and Technology (MinT) is to foster and promote technological advancements, digital innovation, and the effective utilization of information technology across the nation. MinT aims to empower the Ethiopian people through the adoption of cutting-edge technologies, digital literacy programs, and strategic partnerships, thereby driving sustainable economic growth, improving public services, and enhancing the overall quality of life for all citizens

Vision

Our vision is to position Ethiopia as a leading digital nation and a hub for technological innovation in Africa. We envision a future where cutting-edge technologies drive economic prosperity, social development, and environmental sustainability. Through strategic partnerships, research and development initiatives, and a digitally empowered citizenry, we aim to create a knowledge-based society that leverages technology to address national challenges, bridge the digital divide, and unlock the full potential of our nation's human capital.
